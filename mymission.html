My Mission: The Best 2 Years

I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints for 2 years.

  1. Timeline
    • Home MTC: September 2021-October 2021
    • Texas McAllen Mission: October 2021-April 2022
    • Bolivia Santa Cruz Mission: April 2022-August 2023


  2. What do missionaries do?
    • Preach the word of God
    • Learn and teach in foreign languages at a rapid pace
    • Dedicate 18-24 months to serve (they receive no pay)


  3. What I learned
    • How to speak and understand people in the Spanish language
    • How to deal with conflict and strengthen relationships
    • The importance of having a life centered in religious values
    • How to be grateful even in difficult circumstances
    • How to work in a team
